A slide is just markup.

<section class="slide">
  <h2 class="title">Caching cut p99 by 40%.</h2>
  <ul class="bullets">
    <li>One idea per line.</li>
  </ul>
  <aside class="notes">What you say, not what they read.</aside>
</section>

No framework, no JSX, no toolchain. If you can write HTML, you can write a slide — and the classes come from the theme.
